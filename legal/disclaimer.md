**Last updated:** 2026-07-07

## AI-generated content

claude-helm uses Claude (an AI model by Anthropic) to generate content on your behalf. This includes but is not limited to:

- CLAUDE.md project documentation
- README.md files
- Legal documents (privacy policies, terms of use, etc.)
- Refactored code
- Commit messages
- Test files
- Archive documentation

All output is AI-generated. AI models can make mistakes, omit important details, misread context, or produce content that is plausible but incorrect.

## No professional advice

Nothing generated by claude-helm constitutes professional advice of any kind, including but not limited to:

- **Legal advice.** Legal documents generated by `/helm:legal` are starting points for your own review. They are not a substitute for advice from a qualified lawyer. Do not publish or rely on AI-generated legal documents without independent review, especially for commercial or high-stakes products.
- **Financial advice.** No output from this plugin should be treated as financial or investment guidance.
- **Security advice.** Refactored code and safety rules generated by the plugin should be reviewed by a qualified engineer before deployment in security-sensitive contexts.

## Accuracy limitations

The plugin reads your codebase and generates outputs based on what it finds. It may:

- Miss files or context outside its read scope
- Misinterpret code structure or intent
- Generate documentation that does not accurately reflect how the software behaves
- Produce legal documents that do not comply with laws in your jurisdiction
