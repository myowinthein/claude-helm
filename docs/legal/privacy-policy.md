# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-07-07

## Who We Are

claude-helm is an open source plugin for Claude Code, built and maintained by Myo Win Thein. It provides slash commands and rule files that help solo developers ship, refactor, test, and document their own projects. The plugin runs entirely on your local machine and has no server component.

## What Data We Collect

We collect no data. claude-helm does not transmit any information to us, to third parties, or to any remote service.

Specifically, we do not collect:

- Personal information (name, email address, IP address)
- Usage data or analytics
- Project files, code, or any content processed by the plugin
- Crash reports or error logs

## How We Use Your Data

Because we collect no data, there is no processing to describe. No GDPR legal basis applies.

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share no data with any third party. claude-helm itself does not connect to any external service.

Some commands instruct Claude Code to use third-party tools you have independently installed, such as Git and the GitHub CLI (`gh`). These tools operate under their own terms and privacy policies; claude-helm does not control or access any data they handle.

## International Data Transfers

We transfer no data. No personal data leaves your machine via this plugin.

## Data Retention

We retain no data. Nothing is stored by claude-helm outside your local machine.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR Articles 15–21, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to the processing of your personal data. Because we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, delete, or restrict. If you have any questions, contact us using the details below.

## Children's Privacy

claude-helm is a developer tool not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 16.

## Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority under GDPR Article 77. In the EU, a list of national authorities is available at [edpb.europa.eu](https://www.edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/about-edpb/members_en).

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the plugin after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, open an issue at [github.com/myowinthein/claude-helm/issues](https://github.com/myowinthein/claude-helm/issues).
